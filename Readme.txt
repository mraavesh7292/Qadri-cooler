3. HOW TO CUSTOMIZE (Step-by-Step)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. CHANGING BUSINESS DETAILS:
Open 'index.html' in any text editor (VS Code, Notepad++) and find:
- <title>: Change to your business name.
- <h1>: Update the main headline.
- <a> tags with 'tel:': Update with your phone number.
- <a> tags with 'wa.me': Update with your WhatsApp number and custom message.

B. UPDATING PRODUCT IMAGES:
1. Go to the 'images' folder.
2. Replace 'cooler1.jpg', 'cooler2.jpg', etc., with your own product photos.
3. Keep the file names same or update them in the <img> tags in 'index.html'.

C. COLORS & STYLING:
In the <style> section at the top of 'index.html', you can change the
--primary and --dark CSS variables to match your brand colors.
